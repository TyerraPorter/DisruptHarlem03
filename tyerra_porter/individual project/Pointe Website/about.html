About Us

Tyerra is a young woman from the Bronx who has always had a heart for the betterment of herself and her community. She has been an active member of Central Baptist Church for over 10 years where she has volunteered as a Sunday school administrator, dance coach, choir director, prayer counselor and youth minister in her time there. She has contributed to the lives of over 100 children and their families. Her need for individualism and creativity has prompted her to pursue entrepreneurial ventures. She intends to create business opportunities and policies that not only create a productive work environment but encourages the growth of her employees and sets them up to achieve their own goals. Implementing benefits such as in-house day care and scheduled interview dates for her employees encourage a healthy work/life balance that ultimately boosts productivity.
