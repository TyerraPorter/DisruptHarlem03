Pride an Prejudice ia a Victorian novel based in England. The Bennet family is like any other, trying to conform the norms and morays of the time period.

With five daughters of marriagable age the Bennet's are busy finding suitible husbands for them. The eldest two daughters are first up and they have completely different personalities. Jane is more cooperative when it comes to the process. She's level headed, understanding, and beautiful which makes her highly marketable in the marriage pool. Elizabeth, on the other hand, does not see the value in marriage and is very content following her own rules. Lets see if that leads her to love...
