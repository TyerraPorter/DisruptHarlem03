    Elizabeth Bennet is the second of 5 daughters in the Bennet household. Their mother's primary goal is to see that her daughters marry well by any means necessary.

    Elizabeth marches to the beat of her own drum and often avoids her mother advances and inquiries with a witty remark. She is very intelligent and strong willed which makes her her father's favorite. She also becomes a favorite of the Mr. William Darcy, whom she later marries after a series of trying events.
